How Waze retains data we collect
================================

Some data you can delete whenever you like, some data is deleted automatically, and some data we retain for longer periods of time when necessary. This section describes why we hold onto different types of data for different periods of time.

*   **Information retained until you remove it** - such as your “Home, “work”, favorites, email address and other account related information.  We’ll keep this data in your Waze Account until you choose to remove it and/or update it.
*   **Data that expires after a specific period of time** - in some cases, you don’t need to actively delete your information as we set retention timeframes based on the reason for the data’s collection. For example, if you choose to connect your calendar to your Waze Account, we extract your calendar events for several days in advance and delete them after their occurrence. We may also take steps to anonymize certain data within set time periods.
*   **Information retained until your Waze Account is deleted** - we may keep some data for the life of your Waze Account if it’s useful for helping us understand how users interact with our features and how we can improve our services. For example, if you made an incident report, we will keep information about how many reports you have submitted, even after the event is removed. When you delete your Waze Account, the information about how many reports you have submitted is also removed.
*   **Information retained for extended time periods for limited purposes** - sometimes business and legal requirements oblige us to retain certain information, for specific purposes, for an extended period of time. For example, when Waze processes a payment for you, we’ll retain this data for longer periods of time as required for tax or accounting purposes. Reasons we might retain some data for extended periods of time include:
    *   Security, fraud & abuse prevention
    *   Financial record-keeping
    *   Complying with legal or regulatory requirements
    *   Ensuring the continuity of our services
    *   Direct communications with Waze

Ads on Waze
===========

### Ads on Waze

You may see ads as you use Waze. Waze utilizes Google’s advertising technology to select and display these ads, which help keep the Services free of charge. 

Ads on Waze may be shown based on your current Waze activity, such as your approximate location, the general path of your route, your destination type, and other contextual information related to your current drive session. For example, you may see an ad for an open retail store as you drive nearby; or, if you search for “gas station”, you may see an ad for a local gas station.

### Personalized Ads

Depending on your settings, you may also see **ads personalized** to you based on:

*   Information you have provided Waze (for example, your saved “Home” and “Work” addresses)
*   Your past activity and search query history (for example, drives that appear in your drive list)
*   If available based on your device and account settings, information from your Google Account profile and activity.

You can control whether ads are personalized through your Waze in-app **Privacy Settings–>Ads personalization**.

You can control your Google ads personalization settings in [My Ad Center](https://myadcenter.google.com/home). Your Google account is subject to the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). 

Please note:

*   If you turn off ads personalization, you may still see ads on Waze, they will just not be personalized to you.
*   We don’t personalize ads based on sensitive categories, such as race, religion, sexual orientation, or health.
*   We don’t share information that personally identifies you with advertisers, such as your name or email, unless you ask us to. For example, if you see an ad for a nearby flower shop and select the “tap to call” button, we’ll facilitate your call, and we may share your phone number with the flower shop.
*   Advertisers, not Waze, are responsible for the content of their ads. Waze is not responsible for products or services provided by advertisers.

### Ads Measurement

As mentioned above, Waze utilizes Google’s advertising technology to show ads on Waze. Waze may share information with Google to help advertisers understand the performance of their ad campaigns on Waze. For example, Waze may share that a user navigated to a flower shop after viewing their ad on Waze, and Google may share aggregated reporting metrics with advertisers. Waze will not share your personal information with an advertiser unless you ask us to. 

You can learn more about how Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services and how you can control that on [this page](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).
