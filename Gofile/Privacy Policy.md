Privacy Policy
==============

Gofile is designed with privacy at its core. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the rights you have regarding your information.

--------------------

Gofile is an online cloud storage and file sharing platform operated by WOJTEK SAS, a company registered in France. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all individuals who use our Service, and we have designed our platform to collect as little personal data as possible.

This Privacy Policy applies to all Users of the Gofile platform, whether accessing the Service as a guest, a registered free user, or a Premium subscriber. It describes our practices regarding the collection, use, storage, and disclosure of personal data, as well as the choices and rights available to you.

By using Gofile, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of it, you should discontinue use of the Service.

Data We Collect
---------------

Gofile collects only the minimum data necessary to provide the Service. The information we collect depends on how you use the platform:

### Guest Access

When using Gofile as a guest without creating an account, we do not collect any personal data. A temporary session identifier is generated for technical purposes but is not linked to any identifiable individual.

### Free Account

When you create a free account, we collect and store your email address. This serves as your account identifier and is used exclusively to send you secure login links. No password is required or stored. We do not collect your name, phone number, physical address, or any other personal identifiers.

### Premium Account

When you upgrade to Premium, additional billing-related information is collected as necessary to process payments and generate invoices. This may include your name, billing address, and payment method details. For recurring subscriptions, this data is processed by our merchant of record, Number X Europe OÜ. For Pay-as-You-Go and one-shot yearly payments, this data is processed directly by WOJTEK SAS.

### Automatically Collected Data

Like most online services, our servers automatically log certain technical information when you access the platform, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and timestamps of requests. These logs are retained for a limited period and are used exclusively for security, abuse prevention, rate limiting, and platform diagnostics. They are not used for profiling, tracking, or marketing purposes.

Data Sharing & Third Parties
----------------------------

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data to third parties. We do not share personal data with advertisers, data brokers, or analytics companies. Data is only shared in the following limited circumstances:

*   **Payment Processors:** For subscription payments, billing data is processed by our merchant of record, Number X Europe OÜ. For other payment methods, data may be shared with the payment provider strictly as necessary to complete the transaction.
*   **Infrastructure Providers:** Our servers and network infrastructure are operated by trusted hosting providers. These providers process data solely on our instructions and under contractual obligations that ensure equivalent data protection.
*   **Legal Compliance:** We may disclose data when required by law, such as in response to a valid court order, subpoena, or law enforcement request. We carefully review each request and only disclose the minimum data necessary to comply. Where permitted, we notify affected Users before disclosure.

Data Retention
--------------

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law:

*   **Account Data:** Email addresses are retained for the duration of the account's existence. Upon account deletion, the email address is removed from our active systems.
*   **Billing Data:** Invoicing records are retained for the period required by applicable tax and accounting laws (typically 10 years under French law).
*   **Server Logs:** Technical logs containing IP addresses are retained for a short period (generally up to 30 days) and are then automatically purged, unless preservation is required for an ongoing security investigation or legal matter.
*   **User Content:** Files and folders uploaded by Users are retained according to the storage terms applicable to their account tier. Free accounts are subject to ephemeral storage; Premium accounts enjoy permanent storage. Deleted content is removed from our servers in a timely manner.

Data Security
-------------

We implement and maintain appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction:

### Encryption

All data transmitted to and from Gofile is encrypted in transit using TLS. Content stored on our servers is encrypted at rest. We strongly recommend Users encrypt sensitive files locally before uploading for an additional layer of protection.

### Access Control

Access to our servers and infrastructure is strictly limited to authorized personnel. Physical access to our datacenters is monitored, logged, and restricted through multiple layers of security.

### Authentication

Gofile uses a passwordless authentication system. Login links are sent exclusively to the User's registered email address and expire after use. This eliminates the risk of password-based attacks such as credential stuffing and brute force.

### Continuous Improvement

We regularly review and update our security practices to address evolving threats. Our infrastructure is designed with defense-in-depth principles, and we monitor for anomalies that may indicate unauthorized activity.

Cookies & Similar Technologies
------------------------------

Gofile uses only essential cookies and local storage that are strictly necessary for the operation of the platform. These include:

*   **Session & Authentication:** Technical tokens stored in your browser to maintain your login session and remember your preferences (such as selected upload proxy). These are essential for the Service to function.
*   **Guest Sessions:** A temporary identifier stored locally to associate your browser with a guest session for the duration of your visit.

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, analytics cookies, or any third-party cookies. We do not deploy fingerprinting scripts, beacons, or similar tracking technologies.
